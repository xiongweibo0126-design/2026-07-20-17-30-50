Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use TokenBase, we collect:

  • Account information: Email address (optional), account creation timestamp
  • Usage data: API request logs including model used, token counts, and timestamps (for billing purposes)
  • Payment data: Payment transactions are processed entirely by Paddle. We do not store credit card numbers or full payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the API gateway service
  • To calculate billing and manage your account balance
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse of the Service
  • To comply with legal obligations when required

3. Data Sharing

  • Paddle: Payment processing is handled by Paddle as Merchant of Record. Paddle receives necessary transaction data to process payments and handle tax compliance.
  • AI Model Providers: Your API requests (messages/prompts) are forwarded to upstream model providers to generate responses. Each provider's own privacy policy applies to data they receive.
  • We do not sell personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your account data, including encrypted transmission (HTTPS) and secure storage of API keys. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

5. Data Retention

Account data and usage logs are retained for the duration of your active account plus 90 days after account deletion for billing dispute resolution purposes.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. Note that some data must be retained for legal and billing purposes even after deletion requests.

7. Contact

For privacy inquiries, please contact us via the email address provided on our website.
