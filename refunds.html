Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. General Policy

All purchases of prepaid credits on TokenBase are non-refundable. Once credit has been added to your account, it cannot be exchanged for cash or transferred to another account.

2. Exceptions

We will consider refund requests only under the following circumstances:

  • Service outage: If TokenBase experiences a prolonged service disruption exceeding 72 hours where no API calls can be processed, affected users may contact us for partial credit compensation proportional to the downtime duration.
  • Billing error: If you were charged incorrectly due to a technical error on our end, we will issue a correction or refund within 14 business days of verification.
  • Legal requirement: Where required by applicable consumer protection laws, refunds will be processed in accordance with those legal requirements.

3. How to Request a Refund

If you believe your situation falls under one of the exceptions above, please contact us with:

  • Your registered email address or API key identifier
  • The date and amount of the purchase in question
  • A detailed description of the issue

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 5 business days.

4. Account Suspension & Forfeiture

Credits remaining in accounts suspended due to violation of our Terms of Service or Acceptable Use Policy are forfeited and not eligible for refund.

5. Payment Processor Policies

Refunds, if approved, will be processed through Paddle, our payment processor. Paddle's own policies and processing times may apply in addition to ours.

Effective date: July 21, 2026
